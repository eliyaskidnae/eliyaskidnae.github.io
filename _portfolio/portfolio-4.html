--- title: "Autonomous Exploration and Pick-and-Place with Perception-Driven Planning" excerpt: "This project combines planning, perception, localization, and control to enable autonomous exploration and object manipulation in unknown environments. Using a high-resolution 2D LiDAR, the robot builds a map while localizing itself in real time. A Dubins-based RRT* planner generates smooth and collision-free paths for safe navigation. In addition to exploration, the robot detects objects, autonomously approaches and picks them up, places them at a predefined location, and then continues its exploration task. The project demonstrates the integration of autonomous navigation, mapping, and robotic manipulation in a single robotic system.
